If you need Urgent Help

Call 112 or 999 if you or someone you know is about to harm themselves or someone else

Other Support

If you're not in need of urgent support, here are a few things you can do:

Talk to Someone You Trust

Reach out to a parent, guardian, teacher, or close friend. Sharing how you feel can bring immediate relief and support.

Seek Professional Help

Consider talking to a school counselor, therapist, or doctor. They can help you understand your feelings and guide you through your mental health challenges.

Use Online Resources

Many websites, like spunout.ie, childline.ie, or aware.ie, offer confidential support through text, chat, or phone.

Join a Support Group

Look for teen mental health support groups in your area or online, where you can connect with others who understand what you're going through.

Practice Self-Care

Incorporate activities that help you relax, like journaling, exercising, or engaging in hobbies. Self-care is essential for mental wellness.
